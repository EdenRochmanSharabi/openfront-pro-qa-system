Community Guidelines

Help us maintain a welcoming and positive environment for all players

Do's

  • • Be respectful and welcoming to new players
  • • Share strategies and help others improve
  • • Report bugs and provide constructive feedback
  • • Use appropriate channels for different topics
  • • Follow platform-specific rules and guidelines

Don'ts

  • • No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior
